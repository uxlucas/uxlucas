## About me

Lucas is a Senior Product Designer who enjoys turning complex systems into experiences that feel simple, intentional, and sometimes a little bit playful. I've spent the last 6+ years working across SaaS platforms, AI, internal tools, finance and e-commerce, usually somewhere between messy requirements and a blank canvas.

By day, I collaborate with product managers and engineers to untangle flows, define structure, and ship practical solutions. By night, I experiment with visuals, interactions, and creative ideas that might not fit inside a Jira ticket.
